Frequently asked questions

What are your facts based on?

We base our facts mostly on books: published 7 books & films + 1 last named 'Cursed Child'. We take into account forum and Internet articles, also what bloggers say. Moreover, private interviews with J.K.Rowling is considered.

What is Harry Potter's wife and son's names?

It is common fact, that Jeany Weasley become a wife of main hero, and his elder son's name is - Albus-Severus. As he always says to him: "Your name is given in an honor of 2 directors of Hogwarts..."

Is Remus Lupin's son Teddy was close to Harry Potter's son?

Exactly. As facts on the last book say, he visited and even lived in Harry's house. So, all his childhood was with Albus-Severus, and even in Hogwarts, they helped each other.

What was the age of Harry Potter in seventh book?

He was 17. When he got 17, there was operation called "7 Potters", who turned into Harry's look in order to confuse enemies.
